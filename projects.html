💡 Project Ideas

Practice your skills with these beginner → advanced projects.

🔹 Beginner (HTML Only)

  • Personal Bio Page — heading with your name, paragraph about yourself, list of hobbies.
  • Simple Recipe Page — title, ingredients in a list, and steps in numbered order.

🔹 Intermediate (HTML + CSS)

  • Portfolio Card — styled card with your photo, name, and links.
  • Blog Article Page — article text styled with headings/subheadings.
  • Quote Page — centered blockquote styled with CSS.

🔹 Advanced (HTML + CSS + JS)

  • Interactive To-Do List — toggle tasks between “✅ Done” and “❌ Not Done”.
  • Color Changer — button that changes background color when clicked.
  • Simple Quiz Page — multiple-choice buttons that reveal “Correct/Incorrect”.

🔹 Bonus (Semantic HTML)

  • News Article Page — use <header>, <nav>, <article>, <footer>.
  • Mini Portfolio Website — multiple pages (Home, About, Contact) linked with <nav>.
